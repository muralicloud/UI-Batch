10 reasons why California state university Monterey Bay is best!

California State University--Monterey Bay admissions is selective with an acceptance rate of 75%. Half the applicants admitted to CSUMB have an SAT score between 960 and 1170 or an ACT score of 17 and 23. However, one quarter of admitted applicants achieved scores above these ranges and one quarter scored below these ranges. The application deadline is Nov. 30 and the application fee at California State University--Monterey Bay is $70.

Every student completes a capstone project before graduating. This capstone demonstrates the knowledge and skills that you've gained while at CSUMB. It's a chance to show future employers and grad schools everything you're capable of - that you've got a degree and you know how to use it.

Our Service Learning program has been recognized nationally for its work in helping students examine issues of justice, diversity and social responsibility through service learning. Service Learning is required of all students, helping them become committed to public and community service.

CSUMB has the lowest combined tuition and fee structure of all CSUs. We also offer a wide range of scholarships and other financial aid options. So you can focus more on your studies, and less on paying for your education.

CSU Monterey Bay is best known for its small class sizes which benefits students by having more one on one time with each student in the class room. CSUMB also is known for having their students become involved in the community and gain different service learning skill.

Honestly I doubt my school is known for anything specific. It's relatively new; established in 1994. However, most people would say that CSUMB is a small university, almost that of a high school. What surprised me most of my school is that everyone is so friendly and most tend get close to each other. In addition, the majority of classes consist of sizes in which teachers can get to know their students on a personal level.

California State University—Monterey Bay is a public institution that was founded in 1994. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 6,799, and the setting is Suburban. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar. California State University—Monterey Bay's ranking in the 2021 edition of Best Colleges is Regional Universities West, #25. Its in-state tuition and fees are $7,143; out-of-state tuition and fees are $19,023.

This school is best known for their Marine Biology program and its Technology program. CSUMB is known for its small class sizes and its avaliability for on campus housing. It is also known to be located in the one the most beautiful locations in the U.S.

We really don't have any traditions. We are still a really new school. There is Greek Fest though that happens every year. It is a big dance where people wear togas and the Sororities and Fraternities do their strolls.

Our Undergraduate Research Opportunities Center matches undergrad students up with faculty mentors who guide them in original research. This experience makes our students very competitive candidates in graduate school applications and prestigious fellowships.

Be sure with CSU Monterey Bay!
